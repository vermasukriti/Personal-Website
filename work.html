Work

A timeline of roles, transitions, and the through-line across my career.

Royal Bank of Canada (RBC)

July 2023 – Present · Toronto

I joined RBC as an Associate in the Leadership Development Program and now work as a Senior Manager in Strategic Design and Execution. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, finance, and execution, with a focus on solving complex operating problems and translating ambiguity into structured, scalable outcomes.

PwC India

Finance Effectiveness (Consulting) · Bangalore · October 2019 – March 2021

At PwC Bangalore, I worked in finance effectiveness consulting, supporting transformation initiatives that brought together process design, controls, stakeholder alignment, and operational improvement. I left in March 2021 to pursue my MBA in Toronto.

PwC India

Statutory Audit · Ahmedabad · September 2016 – October 2019

I began my career as an articled assistant in statutory audit and later progressed to Senior Associate. This period built my foundation in rigor, accountability, and structured problem-solving, while giving me early experience in managing workstreams, developing judgment, and building trust with teams and clients.

University of Toronto

MBA · Toronto

I moved to Toronto to pursue my MBA at the University of Toronto, a transition that broadened my perspective on leadership, business, and the kind of work I wanted to build my career around.
