Professional

I’m a Chartered Accountant (India) and MBA from the University of Toronto, currently working in Strategy & Execution at RBC. My work focuses on translating complex financial and operational problems into structured, scalable solutions that teams can actually implement.

I’ve built my experience across audit, consulting, and strategy roles at PwC and RBC. In these environments, I’ve worked on cost allocation redesign, KPI benchmarking, liquidity and deposit frameworks, and broader finance transformation initiatives—often in situations where ownership is fragmented and the path forward is unclear.

What I enjoy most is bringing structure to ambiguity. I focus on defining problems precisely, aligning stakeholders across functions, and designing systems that hold up beyond initial implementation. My approach is grounded in clarity, practicality, and follow-through—not just analysis, but execution.

I’m known for being calm in high-pressure environments and for asking the questions that move decisions forward. I care about doing work that is not only correct, but usable, sustainable, and trusted by the people who rely on it.
